Steps you need to follow:

1. At very first 'run the python code'.

2. After running the python code, you will get a link (http://127.0.0.1:5000). Copy this link your chrome tab, you will be redirect to main webpage where you can upload your pdf files.

3. When you reach the main webpage. 'Select your pdf files which you want to convert it into docx.'

4. Tap on 'convert' and then wait for few sec.

5. Finally tap on 'Download in Word' when you see it after few sec, to get your file downloaded in word.
